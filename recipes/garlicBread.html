Garlic Bread

Description

Cripy toast with garlic, butter and fresh herbs. What isn't there to love?

This simple recipe will have your friends begging for more!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian loaf or French loaf
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger (grated, minced, or paste)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or foil. Slice bread in half lengthwise and place on a large baking sheet, cut sides up.
  2. Add the chicken into the yogurt mixture and mix to coat the cubes evenly. Seal the bowl with foil or plastic wrap. Set aside and marinate in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to 2 hours. Better if overnight
  3. In a medium bowl, mix butter, garlic, parsley and salt together until well combined. Spread evenly over bread.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until slightly golden brown on the edges. If you want to add cheese, add it the last 2 minutes of baking. If you like a crispier garlic bread, bake for 3-5 minutes more, watching carefully until it is as golden as you like.
